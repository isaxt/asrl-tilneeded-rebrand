Pilot record · 2024–2025

Housing Works × Altor Safety

Housing Works, a New York City nonprofit providing housing and medical services to homeless New Yorkers, contracted directly with Altor Safety, a U.S. PPE manufacturer located 39 miles from the city, across two consecutive years.
